WELCOME TO FELLINI KOOLINI'S
Fellini Koolini's is a casual fine dining restaurant specializing in Italian and Mediterrainian cuisine located in London, Ontario. We create rustic, honest food that pleases your senses from classic flavours to new, inspired dishes. Menu favourites include an endless selection of made to order pastas, gourmet thin crust pizza, daily fresh fish, steamed mussles, calamari, steak, poultry, pork, veal, vegetarian and gluten-free options.
