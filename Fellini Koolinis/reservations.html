RESERVATIONS

Fellini's offers reservations for parties of six or more people prior to 9 pm.

From company lunches to intimate birthday dinners, you and your guests will enjoy authentic, worldly cuisine in an inviting bistro atmosphere.

Fellini's has three rooms that can accommodate larger parties:

  • The Ball Room, seats 50 people
  • The Front Room, seats 30 people
  • The Library, seats 20 people.

There are also areas in the restaurant that can seat medium-sized parties of 10 to 12 people. Choose from either a set menu, order items a la carte or custom order a specialized menu.
